About DermaScan

DermaScan is an advanced AI-powered platform designed to help identify potential skin conditions through image analysis. Our mission is to make preliminary skin assessments more accessible to everyone.

How It Works

1

Upload Your Image

Take a clear photo of your skin condition and upload it to our secure platform. The clearer the image, the more accurate our analysis will be.

2

AI Analysis

Our advanced machine learning model analyzes your image, identifying patterns and characteristics that match known skin conditions in our database.

3

Review Results

Get instant feedback about potential skin conditions. Remember, our results are informational only and should be followed up with a healthcare professional.

Important Notes

Not a Medical Diagnosis: DermaScan is designed to provide information about potential skin conditions, but it is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Consult Healthcare Professionals: Always consult with qualified healthcare providers for any medical concerns. Our AI analysis should be used as a preliminary tool only.

Privacy: Your privacy is important to us. All images uploaded to DermaScan are processed securely and are not shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Technology Limitations: While our AI model is trained on thousands of images, it may not recognize all skin conditions or may occasionally misidentify conditions that look similar.
