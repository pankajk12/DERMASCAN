How to Use DermaScan

Simple Steps for Skin Analysis

Take a Clear Photo

Ensure good lighting and focus when taking a photo of your skin condition. The clearer the image, the more accurate our analysis will be.

Upload Your Image

Click the "Start Analysis" button on our homepage or navigate to the Analysis page. Drag and drop your image or click to browse your files.

Review Results

After our AI analyzes your image, you'll receive information about potential skin conditions that match your image, along with confidence levels.

Consult a Professional

Use our results as a starting point for discussion with healthcare professionals. Always seek medical advice for proper diagnosis and treatment.

Detectable Skin Conditions

Allergic Reactions

Including poison ivy reactions, contact dermatitis, and other allergic skin responses that cause redness, itching, and inflammation.

Dry Skin Rash

Atopic dermatitis, eczema, and other conditions causing dry, itchy, or flaky skin with potential cracking and inflammation.

Hives

Urticaria and other allergic skin reactions causing raised, itchy welts on the skin that can appear suddenly and change in size and location.

Moles & Skin Cancer

Detection of potentially concerning moles and skin lesions that may require medical attention, including changes in size, color, or shape.

Non-cancerous Growths

Identification of benign skin growths such as skin tags, warts, and seborrheic keratoses that are typically harmless but may cause discomfort.

Pimples & Acne

Analysis of acne conditions, including different types and potential severity levels, from mild comedones to more severe inflammatory acne.
